Cereal

Description

This is a delicious meal that is easily customizable. It is possible to change the essence of this meal simply by substituting one ingredient for another.

This recipe is good for children or people with very little time. With a bit of preparation beforehand, this meal can be made in less than a minute.

Ingredients

  • Storebought Cereal of Choice
  • Optional: Milk
  • Optional: Sugar

Steps

  1. Pour dry cereal in bowl.
  2. Ad desired amount of milk.
  3. Add sugar or other sweetner if desired. For most recipes, this is unrequired.
