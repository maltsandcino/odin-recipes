Crepe

Description

This is a description of crepes and how to make them. Crepes should be fried one by one in a pan, and remain relatively thin. When you have completed cooking them, it is advised to garnish them in some way to complement their basic flavour. You may add whatever type of garnish you wish, frequently a sweet garnish is chosen.

To make the crepes, start by mixing all the dry ingredients. Then, slowly add the wet ingredients. Finally, pour a thin layer in a hot oiled pan and fry until lightly browned. Repeat.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water or Milk
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Mix in wet ingredients
  3. Heat oil in pan
  4. Pour thin layer of batter into pan
  5. Fry and flip, and fry some more
